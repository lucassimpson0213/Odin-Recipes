Recipe Title

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar pasta sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, cook ground beef and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in pasta sauce and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat egg and mix in ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Repeat layers of meat sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture two more times.
  10. Top with remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
